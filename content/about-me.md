Hi, I'm Atalay. I work as a software engineer at [Akamai](https://akamai.com) working on creating/feeding models to improve the efficiency of one of the largest content delivery networks in the world. Previously, I had an internship at [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/) and built tools for digital humanities projects at [Jane Bancroft Cook Library](https://www.ncf.edu/library/) at New College of Florida, where I studied Computer Science and Statistics.

## Projects

### ilacabak
[ilacabak.com](https://ilacabak.com) is Turkey's second most popular medicine information website. The website aims to provide comprehensive and updated information to both consumers and healthcare professionals. Ilacabak serves over a million users every month.
