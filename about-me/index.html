About Me

Hi, I’m Atalay. I work as a software engineer at Akamai working on creating/feeding models to improve the efficiency of one of the largest content delivery networks in the world. Previously, I had an internship at AWS and built tools for digital humanities projects at Jane Bancroft Cook Library at New College of Florida, where I studied Computer Science and Statistics.

Projects#

ilacabak#

ilacabak.com is Turkey’s second most popular medicine information website. The website aims to provide comprehensive and updated information to both consumers and healthcare professionals. Ilacabak serves over a million users every month.
